About Racrovet Skincare

Racrovet Skincare is a clean beauty brand dedicated to nourishing skin and hair with thoughtfully crafted, plant-based formulas. We focus on high-quality oils, butters, herbs, and botanicals that help promote moisture, strength, and overall skin and scalp health.

Each product is vegan, cruelty-free, and made without unnecessary fillers. At Racrovet, self-care is more than a routine, it’s a lifestyle.

Philosophy: Scalp-first care • Consistency over hype • Carefully Made.

Ingredient Safety and Compliance

All ingredients used in Racrovet Skincare products are cosmetic-grade and permitted for use in the United States. Ingredient names follow INCI standards and are listed in descending order of predominance.

Racrovet Skincare maintains ingredient documentation and safety records in accordance with MoCRA requirements.
